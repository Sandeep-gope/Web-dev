Ramayana

Lord Rama is the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu. He is one of the main deities in Hinduism and the central character of the Hindu epic Ramayana. Goddess Sita is his wife.Rama is especially important to Vaishnavism. He is the central figure of the ancient Hindu epic Ramayana, a text historically popular in the South Asian and Southeast Asian cultures.
